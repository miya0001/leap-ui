Easy way to integrate gesture control on your WordPress sites with Leap Motion.

== Description ==

= Supported Gesture =

- Scroll Down: Spin your finger to the right.
- Scroll Up: Spin your finger to the right.
- Scroll to the Top: Swipe your hand.

Coming soon!

- Click hyper links.
- Go to the next articles.
- Custom gesture support.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `plugin-name.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
